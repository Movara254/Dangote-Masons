About Dangote Masons And procurement

Dangote Masons And procurement has been delivering high-quality infrastructure and building projects across Kenya since 2010. Our experienced engineers, architects, and project managers ensure every project meets international standards of safety, quality, and sustainability.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, safe, and efficient construction solutions that empower communities and drive Kenya’s development.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted construction company in East Africa, known for quality, innovation, and integrity.

Our Team & Capabilities

  • Experienced civil, structural, and MEP engineers
  • Certified project managers and safety officers
  • Heavy equipment fleet including excavators, rollers, and pavers
  • In-house logistics and materials management
